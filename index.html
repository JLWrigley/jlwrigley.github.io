Joshua L. Wrigley

I am a logician and category theorist, specialising in topos theory. I am currently a Marie Skłodowska-Curie postdoctoral fellow at Masarykova Univerzita, working with John Bourke on my project HiDimAFOL (Higher Dimensional Aspects of First Order Logic).

Previously, I was a post-doc at Université Paris Cité, working with Sam van Gool, and at Queen Mary University of London, working with Ivan Tomašić. Before that, I completed my PhD under the supervision of Olivia Caramello at the University of Insubria.

My research involves the interaction between mathematical logic, topology, and category theory. I am especially interested in topos-theoretic approaches to model theory.
