Joshua L. Wrigley

I am currently a post-doc of Ivan Tomašić at Queen Mary University of London. In January 2025, I will begin a position working with Sam van Gool at Université Paris Cité.

Before that, I completed my PhD under the supervision of Olivia Caramello at the University of Insubria, studying topos theory.

My research involves the interaction between mathematical logic, topology, and category theory. I am especially interested in topos-theoretic approaches to model theory.
